construction Ideal Prompt Structure

A good prompt is like a recipe. The more precise the ingredients and instructions, the better the result. Here is a proven structure:

[Image Style], [Subject/Scene Description], [Details and Context], [Lighting], [Technical Parameters]

Not all elements are always necessary, but their combination gives the greatest control over the result.

key Keywords & Phrases

These keywords significantly increase the level of photorealism and detail.

  • Quality: photorealistic, hyperrealistic, ultra-detailed, 8K, sharp focus
  • Style: cinematic, film still, DSLR photo, professional photography
  • Rendering Engine: Unreal Engine, V-Ray, Octane Render

lightbulb The Magic of Lighting & Camera Settings

Lighting Types:

  • cinematic lighting - filmic, dramatic
  • golden hour - warm, soft light
  • blue hour - cool light after sunset
  • studio lighting - controlled, professional
  • neon lighting - colorful, urban
  • Rembrandt lighting - classic, portraiture

Camera Settings:

  • wide-angle lens
  • telephoto lens
  • macro lens
  • shallow depth of field - blurry background (bokeh)
  • long exposure

hub Popular AI Image Generation Engines

Each engine has its strengths. It's worth experimenting to find the one that best suits your style.

Midjourney

Known for generating very artistic, polished, and often "opinionated" images. Excellent for creating unique, aesthetic compositions.

Stable Diffusion

An open-source model that offers enormous flexibility and control. It requires more technical knowledge but allows for training custom models and advanced modifications.

DALL-E 3 (OpenAI)

Integrated with ChatGPT, it excels at understanding complex and descriptive prompts in natural language. Ideal for quickly prototyping ideas.

Adobe Firefly

Trained on the Adobe Stock library, making it safe for commercial use. It integrates seamlessly with the Adobe ecosystem (e.g., Photoshop).
